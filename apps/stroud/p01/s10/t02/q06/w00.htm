To find the approximate value m0 we create a spreadsheet with the numbers -10 to 10 with a step value of 1 ranged down the first column and the formula =4 − 2x − xn down the second column (the x here will be replaced by the appropriate cell address in the spreadsheet).
We then draw an X-Y graph and find that the value of x0 lies between 1 and 2.
We then change the numbers in the first column to a range of 0 to 2 with a step value of 0.1 and find that a good approximation to the solution is x0 = 1.2.
