Further problems 1(c) Combinations
There are 12×11×10×9×8 ways of hanging the 5 red hardhats, not all of which are different. This leaves 7 hat pegs so there are 7×6×5×4 ways of hanging the 4 blue hardhats, not all of which are different. This leaves 3 hat pegs so there are 3× 2 ways of hanging the 2 white hardhats, not all of which are different. Each arrangement of 5 red hardhats can be re-arranged 5! times, each arrangement of 4 blue hardhats can then be re-arranged 4! times and each arrangement of 2 white hardhats can be re-arranged 2! times. Furthermore, each re-arrangement is contained within the total number of ways. The total number of different ways is, therefore:
12  ×  11  ×  10  ×  9  ×  8
5 !
 × 
7  ×  6  ×  5  ×  4
4 !
 × 
3  ×  2
2 !
 =  83160
