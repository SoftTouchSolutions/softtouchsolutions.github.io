Given a row of 12 hat pegs, in how many different ways can 5 identical red hardhats be hung?  
There are   12  ×  _  ×  _  ×  _  ×  8  ways of hanging the 5 hardhats,
 not all of which are different.
Each arrangement of 5 hardhats can be re-arranged   _ !  times
 and each re-arrangement is contained within the total number of ways.
The first hardhat can be hung in any one of 12 ways, the next in any one of 11 ways and so on.
