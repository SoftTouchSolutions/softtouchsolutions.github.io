Installing and running the Personal Tutor
Installing the Stroud Engineering Mathematics Personal Tutor

The Personal Tutor does not need to be installed on your computer.

  • Put the CD-ROM in the CD-ROM drive. The Personal Tutor will start automatically.
  • If the Personal Tutor does not start automatically it may be that this feature is disabled on your computer.
    In this case, with the Personal Tutor CD-ROM in the CD-ROM drive, open Windows Explorer (on most computers accessible via 'Start/Programs/Accessories/Windows Explorer'), navigate to the CD-ROM drive and double click on the file 'PersonalTutor.htm'. Your internet browser will open and display the Personal Tutor home page.
Running the Personal Tutor

The Personal Tutor runs in the default internet browser on your computer. You must have an internet browser installed on your computer to be able to use the Personal Tutor.

  • When started from the CD-ROM, the Personal Tutor works entirely offline. Your computer does not need to be connected to the internet.
  • This version of Personal Tutor works on PCs with recent versions of many different types of internet browsers: Internet Explorer (v. 6 and 7), recent versions of Mozilla/Firefox (v. 1.5+), Netscape (v.7.1) and Opera (version 9.02+). Developments in these browsers after the publication of the CD-ROM version of the Personal Tutor may mean that you need to upgrade your copy of the program. See the Contacts section for the link you can use to do this.
  • Because the Personal Tutor runs in your internet browser, you can bookmark its start page just as you would a website.
  • Depending on the type of your browser and its security settings, you may need to manually enable scripts for the Personal Tutor. Consult your browser documentation on how to do this.
Hardware and software requirements for the Personal Tutor

If your computer supports any of the browsers listed above then it will run the Personal Tutor satisfactorily. Your computer should have a display of at least 800 pixels in width, but the larger the display area the easier it will be to work with the Personal Tutor.

If you experience difficulty starting or running the Personal Tutor

Consult the Frequently Asked Questions section. If this does not help you to solve the problem then see the Contacts section for the link you can use to get help.
