Who should use the Personal Tutor and why?
Who should use the Personal Tutor?

Any student studying for a degree in Engineering or Science will benefit from using the Personal Tutor.

What will I learn?

You will learn how to structure a solution to a problem in a logical manner and how to lay it out so that others can follow your reasoning.

The Personal Tutor does not contain any of the explanatory material that is in the book Engineering Mathematics 6th edition. It contains around 700 questions, being a subset of the questions in the book, each of which is broken down into a step-by-step procedure. The Personal Tutor allows you to work through this material at your own pace and as often as you wish.
