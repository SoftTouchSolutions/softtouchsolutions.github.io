Symmetry about the axes
If only  _  powers of  y  occur the graph will be symmetric about the  _ -axis
y  only appears with an  _  power so there is no symmetry about the   _ -axis
If only  _  powers of  x  occur the graph will be symmetric about the  _ -axis
x  only appears with both  _  and even powers so there is no symmetry about the   _ -axis
Consider the oddness and evenness of the powers of x and y to determine any symmetry.
