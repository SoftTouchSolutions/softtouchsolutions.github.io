The centroid of the triangle OAB is denoted by G. If O is the origin and
OA  =  4 i + 3 j
OB  =  6 i − j
Find  OG  in terms of the unit vectors,  i  and  j .
The centroid of the triangle lies on the line joining the midpoint P of side OA to the opposite vertex at B.
Also:
BP  =  BG _ GP
where
BG  =  _ GP
The three medians of a triangle intersect at the centroid which lies one-third the way up each median.
