Using a spreadsheet we enter the values of:
n from 0 to 4 in column A from cell A2 to A6,
xn from x0 to x4 in column B from cell B2 to B6,
f (xn) from f (x0) to f (x4) in column C from cell C2 to C6,
f ′ (xn) from f ′ (x0) to f ′ (x4) in column D from cell D2 to D6.
The formula in cell C2 is = 4 − 2 * C2 − C2 ^ 3  copied down to C6
The formula in cell D2 is = − 2 − 3 * ( C2 ^ 2 )  copied down to D6
The formula in cell B3 is = B2 − C2 / D2  copied down to B6
The number in cell B2 is 1.2 and the resulting spreadsheet is, with all results displayed to 7 decimal places:
